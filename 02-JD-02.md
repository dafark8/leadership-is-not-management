# Job Description - Sample 2 - UX Team Manager

## Position Summary

As a UX Team Manager, you will be responsible for orchestrating and empowering a dedicated team of User Experience (UX) researchers and designers. You will play a pivotal role in fostering a culture of user-centric design and innovation, shaping the future direction through the insights and findings your team delivers.

## Who You Will Work With:
You will lead a multi-disciplinary team of UX practitioners, working closely with designers, product managers, and engineers who are passionate about creating exceptional user experiences. You will collaborate with stakeholders across various departments, including Marketing, Sales, and Engineering. Your team's actions and deliverables will include:

- Facilitating user research and delivering actionable insights.
- Exploring and delivering designs and design related solutions included journey maps, personas, and workflows
- Organizing workshops and collaborative sessions to generate ideas and concepts.
- Fostering strong relationships with stakeholders to ensure alignment between their expectations and the product design and user experience.


## What You'll Do:

As a UX Team Manager, your main responsibilities will include:

- Leading the development and implementation of UX strategies.
- Empowering and guiding your team in conducting user research activities such as user interviews, surveys, and usability studies.
- Leading design critiques and guiding your team to innovative and inclusive solutions. 
- Reviewing and analyzing feedback and activity to enhance the user experience.
- Facilitating the creation of user personas, customer journey maps, and experience maps by your team.
- Collaborating with product teams to ensure the implementation of user-centric designs.
- Communicating to all relevant stakeholders and providing recommendations to enhance product design.

## Who You Are:

### Expected Qualifications:

- A Bachelor's degree in UX design, Psychology, HCI, or related field.
- At least 10-12 years of professional experience in a UX research role, with a significant portion in a leadership capacity.
- Proven experience in guiding and leading a team of UX practitioners.
- Extensive experience in both consulting and private/in-house environments.
- Exceptional communication, presentation, and interpersonal skills.
- Proficiency in UX research tools and methodologies.
- A strong understanding of both qualitative and quantitative research methods, with a recognition that individuals tend to lean towards one area or the other. You should have the ability to balance the strengths of your team members to ensure comprehensive, insightful research results.
- Familiarity with design systems and patterns, and able to coach designers in their craft. 

### Preferred Qualifications:

- A Master’s degree in UX design, Psychology, HCI, or related field.
- Experience working in a global technology company.
- Demonstrated project management skills and ability to coordinate multiple tasks.
- Ability to work independently and take initiative in a dynamic environment.

This role is an excellent opportunity for a seasoned UX Leader who is passionate about fostering a culture of user-centric design and innovation, and is ready to lead and empower a team of UX practitioners at a global level. You understand the unique balance between research, design, and other disciplines and are adept at guiding your team to leverage their individual strengths for the best results.

*One caveat, I try not to rely to strongly on academic training and background. They are often needed for ATS and company filtering, though I work with my recruitment team to find candidates with a diverse set of backgrounds*
